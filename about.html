About Campus Swap

Campus Swap is a student-driven marketplace designed to help college students buy, sell, and exchange goods easily within their campus community.

Our mission is to reduce waste, promote reuse, and provide students with an affordable and sustainable shopping alternative.

What We Offer

  • Buy and sell used goods safely
  • QR-based secure payments
  • Seller verification
  • Admin-managed platform
